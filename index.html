Who I Am

I am Alexi Liszt, and I am a Game Developer. My interests are playing video games, watching movies, drawing and coloring, and playing sports with Special Olympics. I want to work for the Gaming Industry because I play a lot of video games.

What I Do

What I want to do in Game Development is create my own levels and playtest them. I am a passionate designer in which I create a lot of my own characters, environmental objects, and pickups. I create my own levels by placing characters, environmental objects, and pickups wherever I want to. Super Mario Maker inspired me to create my own Mario courses and upload them online. I am most proud of designing object concepts and texture maps in Photoshop, 3D Character modelling and animation in 3DS Max, and creating my own levels in Unity.
